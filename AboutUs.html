About Us

MyBank is a leading private sector bank in India. The Bank’s consolidated total assets stood at Rs. 14.76 trillion at September 30, 2020. MyBank currently has a network of 5,288 branches and 13,723 ATMs across India.

History

MyBank was originally promoted in 1994 by MyBank Limited, an Indian financial institution, and was its wholly-owned subsidiary.
